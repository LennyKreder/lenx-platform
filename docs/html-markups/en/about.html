About Layouts by Lenny

Beautiful digital planners, designed with care.

Layouts by Lenny was founded with a simple mission: creating digital planners that are both beautiful and functional. We believe that planning your life should be a pleasant experience, not a chore.

Our Mission

Helping people organize their lives with thoughtfully designed digital planning tools that work seamlessly across all devices.

Quality & Ease of Use

Every planner is carefully designed with attention to detail, giving you optimal readability and ease of use in GoodNotes, Notability and other note-taking apps.

Minimalist Design

No motivational quotes, no sticker sections, no moon phase trackers. Just clean layouts with space for your thoughts.

Available in 2 Languages

Planners with localized holidays, week numbers and date formats in English and Dutch
