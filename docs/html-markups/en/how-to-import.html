How to Import Your Planner

Follow these simple steps to start using your digital planner in your favorite note-taking app.

GoodNotes

  1. 1Download your planner PDF to your device
  2. 2Open GoodNotes and tap the + button to create a new document
  3. 3Select "Import" and choose your downloaded PDF file
  4. 4Your planner is ready to use! Tap any hyperlink to navigate between pages

Noteshelf

  1. 1Download your planner PDF to your device
  2. 2Open Noteshelf and tap "Create New"
  3. 3Select "Import PDF" and locate your planner file
  4. 4Open your imported planner and start planning!

Notability

  1. 1Download your planner PDF to your device
  2. 2Open Notability and tap the import icon (arrow pointing down)
  3. 3Choose your PDF file from Files or your download location
  4. 4Your planner will open with all hyperlinks working

Tips for Best Experience

  • •Make sure to download the PDF to your device before importing
  • •Keep the original PDF file as a backup
  • •Use the hyperlinks to quickly navigate between sections
  • •Set your planner as a favorite for quick access
